Customer Service Executive

Role Overview

The Customer Service Executive is responsible for providing exceptional service to customers, addressing inquiries, resolving issues, and ensuring customer satisfaction. This role involves effective communication and problem-solving skills to handle customer requests efficiently.

Qualifications

Minimum Qualification: High School Diploma or equivalent.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Excellent communication skills, both verbal and written.
  • Strong interpersonal skills and a customer-oriented mindset.
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment.
  • Prior experience in customer service is a plus but not mandatory.
